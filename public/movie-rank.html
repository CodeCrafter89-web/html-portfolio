Favourite Movies of all time according to Nejc

My Top 3 movies of all time.

(Seven)Se7en

Se7en (1995): A dark psychological thriller directed by David Fincher, "Se7en" follows two detectives (played by Brad Pitt and Morgan Freeman) as they hunt down a serial killer who uses the seven deadly sins as the theme for his murders. The film is known for its intense atmosphere, shocking twists, and exploration of morality and justice.

The Wolf of Wall Street

Directed by Martin Scorsese, this biographical black comedy tells the story of Jordan Belfort (played by Leonardo DiCaprio), a stockbroker who rises to immense wealth through corruption and fraud. The film portrays his excessive lifestyle, including wild parties, drug abuse, and the eventual downfall that follows his unethical actions.

Gladiator

Directed by Ridley Scott, "Gladiator" is an epic historical drama that follows the journey of Maximus Decimus Meridius (played by Russell Crowe), a Roman general who is betrayed and reduced to slavery. He rises through the ranks of the gladiatorial arena to seek revenge against the corrupt emperor who murdered his family. The film is celebrated for its grand scale, gripping action, and powerful performances.
